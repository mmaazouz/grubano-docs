## The principle (and the why)

Influencer status recognizes your ability to effectively recommend restaurants to your community. Unlike a standard [affiliate](/en/guides/affiliate/) who shares links occasionally, a verified influencer can build **structured partnerships** with several restaurants: join their programs, promote specific campaigns, and benefit from content creation tools designed for regular output.

Audience verification assures restaurants that you have a real, engaged community. It adds to your affiliate status — you keep your referral code and your existing links — and simply opens new doors.

## Request verification

<Steps>

### Have an active affiliate account

You must first be registered as a Grubano affiliate. Your affiliate profile gives you a referral code and a referral link.

### Submit your audience application

From your affiliate dashboard, go to the verification form. You provide your main platform (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok or other), your public handle, your declared follower count, and optionally a proof link.

### Wait for the admin review

A Grubano admin reviews your application: account authenticity, community engagement, alignment with the platform's values. The turnaround depends on the volume of requests.

### Receive the decision

If your application is approved, your profile moves to **verified** status: an "Influencer" badge appears on your dashboard. If it is rejected, you can submit a new request after adjusting your profile.

</Steps>

## Maximize your chances

<Callout type="default">

- **Complete public profile**: clear bio, recognizable profile photo, regular posts.
- **Real engagement**: favor an engaged community (comments, shares) over an artificially inflated follower count.
- **Thematic consistency**: your content touches on food, local cuisine, restaurant outings, or a related space.
- **Solid proof link**: provide a recent screenshot of your audience stats or a link to a representative video.

</Callout>

<Eyebrow icon="error">Mistakes to avoid</Eyebrow>

## Common pitfalls

<Callout type="error">

- **Artificial inflation**: buying followers or views hurts your credibility and leads to rejection.
- **Private or inactive profile**: a locked account or one with no recent posts makes it impossible to review your audience.
- **Inconsistency**: declaring 50,000 followers when your profile shows 500 invalidates the application immediately.
- **Multiple simultaneous requests**: you can only submit one request at a time. Wait for the decision before reapplying.

</Callout>

## Prepare your content ahead of time

Once verified, you will have access to a creation studio that automatically generates post captions for the restaurants you recommend. Even before verification, note the local restaurants you love: you will be able to promote them as soon as you are approved, without wasting time.
