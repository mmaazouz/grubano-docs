# Switching to Pro

**Grubano Standard is free.** You receive your orders, you manage your
menu, you chat with your customers — everything is included, and the commission remains
at **10%**.

The **Grubano Pro** subscription (€29/month) is optional and **with no commitment**.
**The commission does not change**: 10% for both plans.

## Standard vs Pro Comparison

| | **Standard** | **Pro** |
|---|---|---|
| **Pricing** | Free | **€29/month** |
| **Commission per order** | 10% | **10%** _(identical)_ |
| Unlimited orders & menu | ✓ | ✓ |
| Customer reviews | ✓ | ✓ |
| Customer data = restaurant’s property | ✓ | ✓ |
| No commitment | — | **✓** |

## What’s Coming Soon with Pro

Grubano Pro will allow you to receive orders from all your other
platforms (Uber Eats, Deliveroo, Just Eat…) directly on your Grubano
dashboard: a single screen for all your orders, unified analytics
and consolidated reports. This multi-platform aggregation
is coming **soon** in Pro — without touching the commission or the base
pricing.

## The Honest Point

<Callout type="info">
  **Pro never increases the commission.** The Grubano commission is **10%**
  in Standard and remains at **10%** in Pro. Pro is a monthly subscription that
  is added on top, it does not take anything extra from your sales.
</Callout>

## No Commitment

The Pro subscription is **monthly with no commitment period**. You activate it
whenever you want and you cancel it whenever you want: the cancellation takes
effect at the end of the current monthly cycle, and your menu, your order
history and your customer base remain intact when switching back to Standard.
