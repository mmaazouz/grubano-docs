# Go Pro

**Grubano Standard is free**: you receive your orders, manage your menu and build
customer loyalty, with a commission based on the **grid by mode** (5 to 12%
depending on the order mode). The **Grubano Pro** subscription (29 €/month) is
optional, no commitment, and **never touches that commission**.

## Multi-platform aggregation

Grubano Pro will let you receive orders from **all your other platforms**
(Uber Eats, Deliveroo, Just Eat…) directly on your Grubano dashboard: a single
screen for all your orders, unified analytics and consolidated reports. This
multi-platform aggregation is coming **soon** to Pro — without touching the
commission or the base price.

<Eyebrow icon="balance">The model</Eyebrow>

## The commission never changes

<Callout type="info">
  **Pro never raises the commission.** The grid by mode (delivery 12%,
  click & collect 8%, dine-in 5%) is identical on Standard and on Pro. Pro
  is a monthly subscription that adds on top; it takes nothing extra from
  your sales.
</Callout>

<Eyebrow icon="lock_open">No commitment</Eyebrow>

## Cancel whenever you want

The Pro subscription is **monthly with no commitment period**. You activate it
whenever you want and cancel it whenever you want: cancellation takes effect at
the end of the current monthly cycle, and your menu, your order history and your
customer base stay intact when you switch back to Standard. **Your customer
relationship and your loyalty remain yours** in both plans.
