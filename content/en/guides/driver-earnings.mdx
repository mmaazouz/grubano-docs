## The principle (and the why)

Every time you deliver an order, the system automatically records your earning: the share of the delivery fees paid by the customer that comes back to you, minus the 20% Grubano commission. If the customer leaves a tip, it is paid out to you in full, with no deduction whatsoever.

To protect everyone involved, your earnings stay *pending* for a short maturation period: this guarantees that the order was properly fulfilled and that no exceptional refund cancels it. Once this delay has passed, the amount moves into your *available* balance and you can withdraw it. This mechanism ensures trust and transparency between you, the restaurants, and Grubano.

## Track and withdraw your earnings

<Steps>

### Check your dashboard

Log in to your courier space and go to the "My earnings" page. There you will see three balances: *available* (what you can withdraw right away), *pending* (earnings still maturing), and *already paid out* (the history of your withdrawals).

### Review the ride details

Each line shows the ride delivered, the gross amount (delivery fee or tip), the Grubano commission where applicable, and the net that comes back to you. The status shows whether the earning is still *pending*, *matured* (available), or *paid* (already transferred).

### Request a withdrawal

As soon as your available balance reaches or exceeds 25 €, the "Withdraw" button becomes active and records your request. The actual triggering of transfers is coming soon: once enabled, the transfer will go to the account you registered when you signed up, with no withdrawal fees.

### Receive the transfer

Once actual triggering is enabled, the payment will arrive within 1 to 3 business days. You will get an email notification when the transfer has been sent, then a second one when your bank has credited it. The history keeps a record of every withdrawal (date, amount, transfer reference).

</Steps>

## Common pitfalls

<Callout type="error">
- **Don't confuse the "pending" balance with the "available" balance**: only the latter can be withdrawn; the wait is a legal security period, not an arbitrary hold.
- **Don't request several withdrawals in quick succession**: if a transfer is already in progress, the "Withdraw" button stays greyed out until the previous one is finalized (protection against duplicates).
- **Don't forget to verify your identity**: a verified bank account (KYC) is required before the first withdrawal — complete this step as soon as you sign up so your earnings don't get blocked.
</Callout>
