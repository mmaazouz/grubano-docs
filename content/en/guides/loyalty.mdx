# Loyalty Program

Turn every euro spent into loyalty points, with four reward tiers that encourage your customers to come back.

<FlowDiagram steps={["Register a customer","Purchase","Point credit","Reward"]} />

## The essentials

Grubano’s loyalty program automatically rewards your customers: **1 point = 1 € spent**. Points accumulate across four tiers (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum) and convert into credit usable on their next order. Points are credited as soon as the order is delivered.

## How it works

Your regular customers deserve recognition. The loyalty program builds a lasting relationship: every purchase strengthens attachment to your brand, every tier reached motivates the next order.

The system works end-to-end — quick registration (name, email, optional phone), automatic validation upon delivery, **conversion of points into euros of credit** that the customer spends whenever they want. You track activity from your [dashboard](/en/guides/restaurant/): point balance, current tier, history of the last ten validated orders.

## Step by step

<Steps>

### Register a customer in the program

Collect the customer’s name and email (phone is optional). **Welcome bonus: 10 points** credited immediately upon account creation. Each customer receives a unique referral code (eight characters, uppercase) they can share.

### Validate a delivered order

When an order is marked **delivered**, enter the customer’s email, order number (unique identifier — never validated twice), and total amount including tax. The system awards **1 point per euro spent** (rounded down to the nearest whole number) and recalculates the tier.

### Track balance and tier

Four progressive tiers unlock access to credit:

- **Bronze** — 0 to 99 points (starting tier)
- **Silver** — 100 to 199 points
- **Gold** — 200 to 399 points
- **Platinum** — 400 points and above

The tier updates automatically as soon as a threshold is crossed. The customer sees their point balance **converted into euros of credit** (conversion rate set by Grubano) that they can spend as they wish.

### Spend the credit

The customer chooses to apply all or part of their credit to the basket. The discount is deducted from the order total; the point balance decreases accordingly. No financial charge on the restaurant side — **Grubano fully finances** the applied discount.

</Steps>

## Best practices

- **Share the referral code** — encourage your customers to share it (business card, packaging, social media). A referred customer earns additional points for the referrer.
- **Remind about the balance** — mention the number of points earned on the receipt or in the confirmation email. An informed customer comes back faster.
- **Target the tiers** — a customer with 180 points knows they have 20 € left to spend to reach Gold. This visible threshold triggers a "milestone" order.
- **Validate quickly** — points are only credited upon actual delivery of the order. The faster the validation, the more the customer perceives the immediate reward.
