# The Affiliate Program

## The Essentials

The affiliate program allows you to recommend Grubano through a personal link or code and earn commission on the orders you generate. You track your earnings in real time and request a withdrawal once the threshold is reached.

## How It Works

Grubano compensates partners who recommend the platform: you share your affiliate link on your networks, your site or with your community, and every new order placed by a customer who arrived via your link earns you a commission. The program is open to everyone — no audience size requirement or subscription prerequisite.

You instantly receive a short code and a personalized link when you sign up. Commission is credited automatically upon delivery of each order, then becomes available after a security period. You track clicks, conversions and earnings on your dashboard, and request a withdrawal when your balance reaches the threshold — actual payment arrives soon.

[Verified influencers](/en/guides/influencer/) — a tier with certified audience — enjoy additional benefits (increased commission, AI content studio), but keep the same affiliate mechanics.

## Step by Step

<Steps>

### Apply to the Program

Visit the public affiliate program page. Fill out the form (name, email address, consent): you will receive a passwordless login link by email.

If you already have a Grubano account, log in directly — you join the program with one click from your dashboard.

### Get Your Links and Codes

As soon as you first log in, the system automatically generates a **short code** (for example `ALEX9F2K`) and a **personalized link** (`/ref/alex9f2k`). Both point to Grubano restaurant discovery and attribute orders to your account.

You can find them at any time on your affiliate dashboard. Copy them, share them as you wish — social networks, personal site, newsletter, community.

### Share and Generate Sales

Every time a visitor clicks on your link or uses your code during their first order, the system records the connection. If that customer places an order within 90 days, commission is automatically credited to you upon delivery.

You track in real time the number of clicks, conversions and the amount of your earnings (pending or available) on your dashboard. The conversion rate is displayed to adjust your strategy.

### Withdraw Your Earnings

As soon as your available balance reaches **€25**, the "Withdraw" button activates on your dashboard. On your first withdrawal, the system asks you to verify your identity (regulatory procedure) and complete a simplified tax declaration — no payment occurs before this step.

Once validated, your request is recorded; actual trigger of transfers arrives soon, and your payment will then be sent within a few business days. You can view the history of your withdrawals at any time; each request is idempotent (a double-click does not trigger two payments).

</Steps>

## Concrete Example

You share your link `/ref/alex9f2k` in the Instagram story of your local food account. Fifteen people click, three place orders within the week. Each of these three orders earns you a commission (calculated on Grubano fees for the order), credited automatically upon delivery. You see "15 clicks · 3 conversions · 20%" on your dashboard, and the corresponding amount is added to your pending balance. Thirty days later, this balance becomes available; if you have accumulated €25 or more, you request withdrawal with one click.

## Best Practices

- **Target local audiences** — Grubano is a marketplace of local restaurants: your recommendations are all the more effective if your community lives in a covered area.
- **Vary formats** — try story, pinned post, link in bio, newsletter: the dashboard shows you which channel converts best (clicks vs orders).
- **Highlight a specific restaurant** — you can build an affiliate link pointing directly to a restaurant’s page (`/ref/alex9f2k?restaurant=<id>`) to recommend an address you love: commission stays the same, conversion often increases.
- **Track your streak** — the dashboard displays a "weekly streak" (number of consecutive weeks with at least one generated order) and progress badges: these gamification mechanics help you maintain a regular rhythm.
