## The principle (and the why)

Your customer account is the heart of your Grubano experience: it brings together your contact information, your delivery and billing addresses, your favorite restaurants, the reviews you've left, and your language preferences. Every order you place, every [loyalty](/en/guides/consumer-loyalty/) point you earn is automatically linked to your profile, which lets you find your history, easily reorder your favorite dishes, and benefit from your loyalty credit.

The "My account" section gives you direct access to all this data. You can view it, edit it, or delete it at any time. The restaurant sees your history and preferences to serve you better — never your contact details (email, phone, address), which Grubano protects; only the information needed for delivery is shared with it for the duration of the order.

## View and edit your account

<Steps>

### Go to the My account page

From the mobile app or the website, tap the profile icon in the navigation bar (bottom right on mobile, top right on desktop). Select **My account**.

### Review your information

You immediately see your **name**, your **phone**, and your **email address**. These fields are pre-filled with the data you provided at sign-up or during your last update.

### Edit your contact details

Tap the field you want to change (name or phone), enter the new value, then tap **Save**. The change is applied instantly.

### Change your email address

For security reasons, changing your email happens in **two steps**:
- Tap **Change email**, enter the **confirmation code** that was sent to your current address, then provide the **new address**.
- A confirmation link will be sent to this new address; click it to finalize the change. Your old address stays active until this validation.

### Manage your delivery addresses

Tap **Addresses** to see the list of all saved addresses. You can add a new one, or edit or delete existing ones. The address marked as **primary** will be offered by default on your next orders.

### Check your favorites

The **Favorites** section displays the restaurants and dishes you've marked with a heart. Tap a favorite to go straight to its page and order again.

### Reread your reviews

The **My reviews** page lists all the comments you've left on restaurants or dishes. You can edit or delete them if you wish.

### Change the app language

In **Settings**, select **Language** and choose from the available languages (French, English, Spanish, Arabic, Italian). The interface is translated instantly.

</Steps>

## Keep your profile up to date

<Callout type="default">
- **Check your phone number**: couriers use it to contact you if needed.
- **Save several addresses** if you regularly order at the office, at home, or at friends' and family's: you'll save time on every order.
- **Mark your favorite dishes** to find them quickly and receive personalized recommendations.
- **Leave constructive reviews**: they help other customers choose and let restaurateurs improve.
</Callout>

<Eyebrow icon="error">Mistakes to avoid</Eyebrow>

## Common pitfalls

<Callout type="error">
- **Not confirming the email change**: if you enter a new address but don't click the confirmation link, your old address stays active and you risk no longer receiving order notifications.
- **Forgetting to update your phone** after a number change: the courier won't be able to reach you if there's a problem.
- **Using an email you no longer check**: you'll miss order summaries and personalized offers.
</Callout>

## Save time when ordering

<Callout type="info">
If you often order to the same addresses, save them all in your profile and name them clearly ("Home", "Office", "Parents"). When you check out your cart, you'll just need to select the address from the list instead of re-entering it each time.
</Callout>
