## The principle (and the why)

Every time you [order](/en/guides/consumer/) and the restaurant delivers, Grubano records your order in your loyalty history and credits your points balance. Those points then turn into loyalty credit in euros — real spending power you use to lower the amount of a future order, no strings attached.

You also build up seniority: the higher your balance grows, the further you climb the tiers (Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum). Your progress is visible at any time on your account, along with how far you are from the next tier.

Finally, you have a personal referral code: share it with friends and family to give them a welcome discount on their first order. Grubano records the link between you and the person you referred, which unlocks perks for you as they keep ordering.

## Refer a friend

<Steps>

### Find your code

On your account page, a "Referral" section shows your personal code (eight uppercase characters). This code is unique and permanent.

### Share it

Send this code to your friends and family through the channel of your choice (message, email, social media). They will need to enter it or follow a link containing this code when they place their first order.

### Your referee gets a welcome discount

When the person you referred validates their first order with your code, Grubano automatically applies a discount to the cart amount (a capped percentage, within the limits set by Grubano). This discount appears in the summary before payment.

### You get credited

Once the order is delivered, Grubano records the referral link between you and your referee. The financial terms of this referral are set between the parties involved; you will see your perks appear as your referee keeps ordering.

</Steps>

## A 240-point journey

You have earned 240 points across your orders. Your balance converts into loyalty credit of 12 € (the conversion rate is set by Grubano). You are at the Gold tier (crossed at 200 points) and you have 160 points left to earn to reach Platinum (400 points). On your next order, you can choose to use all or part of this 12 € to lower the amount to pay — the unused credit stays available.

## Get the most out of your loyalty

<Callout type="default">
- **Order regularly** — every delivered order feeds your balance; even small amounts count.
- **Watch your thresholds** — if you are close to a new tier, one extra order may be enough to cross it.
- **Share your code widely** — the more people you refer, the more chances you create to enjoy perks on their orders.
- **Use your credit whenever it suits you** — you are never required to spend it all at once; the balance stays available as long as your account is active.
</Callout>

<Eyebrow icon="error">Mistakes to avoid</Eyebrow>

## Common pitfalls

<Callout type="error">
- **Not checking your balance before ordering** — you risk paying full price when you had loyalty credit ready to use.
- **Sharing a code that isn't yours** — only your personal code earns you perks; passing along someone else's code credits you nothing.
- **Forgetting to enter a referral code on your first order** — the welcome discount applies only once, on the very first order; you won't be able to claim it retroactively.
</Callout>
