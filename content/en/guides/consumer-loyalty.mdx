# Loyalty, wallet & referral

Earn points on every order, climb loyalty tiers, and share your referral code to turn your purchases into tangible benefits.

## The essentials

The Grubano loyalty program rewards every order: you earn points that convert into credit in euros, redeemable on your next orders. The more you order, the higher you climb through the tiers (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum) and the more your wallet grows. You can also [refer](RELATED_LINKS[0]) friends and family using your personal code: they receive a welcome discount, and you earn points on their purchases.

## How it works

Every time you [place an order](/en/guides/consumer/), your loyalty account accumulates points proportional to the amount spent. These points feed your **wallet**: credit in euros that you spend freely on your next purchases. No fixed rewards or gift catalogs — your point balance converts to euros at a transparent rate, and you choose when to use it.

**Loyalty tiers** (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum) reflect your cumulative history: the more you order, the higher you progress. Each tier marks a visible milestone of your commitment, without changing the conversion rate — all customers benefit from the same credit per point.

**Referral** complements the program: you receive a unique personal code that you share with friends and family. When a referral places their first order with your code, they get a welcome discount, and you earn points on their future purchases for a defined period. It’s a virtuous circle: your friends discover Grubano with an immediate benefit, and you’re rewarded for word of mouth.

## Step by step

<Steps>

### Earn points on every order

With every validated order, your loyalty account automatically receives points proportional to the amount spent. No action required — the system records your earnings once delivery or pickup is complete.

### Check your wallet

Go to your **Account** page in the app: you’ll see your point balance, the corresponding credit in euros, and the conversion scale (for example 100 points = 5 €, 200 points = 10 €, 400 points = 20 €). The history of your transactions — points earned, credit spent, refunds — is accessible in real time.

### Climb the tiers

Your cumulative point total moves you up through the tiers: **Bronze** (0+), **Silver** (100+), **Gold** (200+), **Platinum** (400+). The Account page displays your current tier and the number of points remaining to reach the next one. The tier is a loyalty marker — the conversion rate remains identical for everyone.

### Refer friends and family

Copy your referral code from the Account page and share it by message, social media, or link. When a referral signs up and orders with this code, they receive a discount on their first order, and you earn points on their purchases for a set duration (typically 90 days). Each active referral feeds your wallet.

### Spend your credit

When finalizing an order, the cart offers to apply all or part of your loyalty credit. Select the amount to use — the order total is reduced accordingly. Your point balance decreases, and the transaction appears in your history.

## Best practices

- **Check your history regularly**: every point earned and spent is tracked with the associated order reference. This gives you a clear view of your wallet’s evolution.
- **Share your code as soon as your friends and family sign up**: the welcome discount applies only to the referral’s first order. Remember to give them the code before they create their account.
- **Use your credit strategically**: nothing requires you to spend all your points at once. Save your credit for a large order or divide it according to your needs — you decide.
- **Verify referral eligibility**: the welcome discount applies only to new customers who have never ordered on Grubano. A friend already registered won’t be able to benefit from your code.

## Common mistakes

<Callout type="warning">
**Points not credited**: if you don’t see your points after an order, check that it’s marked "delivered" or "picked up" in your history. Points are credited only once the order is finalized, never at the time of payment.
</Callout>

**Referral code not recognized**: the code must be entered **before** the referral’s first order. If your friend has already ordered once without a code, they can no longer benefit from the welcome discount — the system allows only one referral per account.

**Tier / credit confusion**: moving up a tier (Bronze → Silver) doesn’t change the points/euros conversion rate. The tier is a progress marker, not a value multiplier — all customers convert their points at the same rate.

## Tips & advice

- **Track your progress toward the next tier**: the Account page shows how many points you need to accumulate before reaching Silver, Gold, or Platinum. It’s a good indicator of where you stand in your loyalty journey.
- **Share your personalized link**: instead of copying and pasting a raw code, use the automatically generated referral link — your friends click, sign up, and the code is applied effortlessly.
- **Combine loyalty credit and promotions**: loyalty credit applies to your cart total after restaurant discounts. You can therefore combine a restaurant promotion and your Grubano credit on the same order.
