## The principle (and the why)

Grubano connects **local** restaurants — the ones that cook in their own kitchen — with customers in their neighborhood. You order directly from the restaurant owner, who keeps [their customer relationship and loyalty](/en/guides/consumer-loyalty/) in the app. Real-time tracking works for all three modes: **delivery** (a courier brings you the order), **click & collect** (you pick it up at the restaurant), or **dine-in** (you eat at the table). Each mode has its own commission — the restaurant owner keeps the rest.

Status transparency (received, preparing, ready, on the way, delivered) ensures you're always kept informed. If a restaurant is closed when you order, the app shows you the next opening time and blocks checkout — so you can never place an order that goes nowhere.

## Place and track an order

<Steps>

### Find a restaurant

Browse the map or the list of open restaurants. They show their **estimated delivery time**, their **rating** and their **minimum order**. You can filter by cuisine type and sort by rating or time.

### Build your cart

Add the dishes you like. For each dish, customize the **options** (size, extras, exclusions) and leave a **note** if needed. The cart calculates the total in real time — dish subtotal, delivery fees and any discounts.

### Choose how to receive your order

At the top of the cart, switch between **🛵 Delivery** and **🥡 Click & collect**. Click & collect removes the delivery fees and shows the restaurant's address. Delivery shows your saved address — change it if needed in [your account](/en/guides/consumer-account/).

### Pay online

Check out. Your card is charged immediately, but the money stays **held** until delivery is confirmed — that's a safety guarantee. If the order is cancelled, you get a **full refund**.

### Track in real time

As soon as the restaurant accepts, you receive a notification. The statuses follow one after another: **Preparing** → **Ready** → **On the way** (delivery) or **Picked up** (click & collect) → **Delivered**. A unique tracking link lets you refresh the page at any time.

### Confirm receipt

When the status changes to **Delivered**, the money is released to the restaurant owner. You earn **loyalty points** (1 point per euro spent) that you can spend on your next orders.

</Steps>

## A €25 delivery order

Let's say you order €25 of dishes from a local restaurant, for **delivery**. The restaurant shows a delivery fee of €1.99. No active promotion.

Behind the scenes, Grubano takes a **12% commission on the €25 of dishes** (i.e. €3), never on the delivery fee. The restaurant therefore collects **€22 + €1.99 = €23.99** net. If you had chosen **click & collect**, the commission would have been 8% (€2) and the delivery fee €0, for a total of **€25 to pay** and **€23 net** for the restaurant. Courier tips are passed on in full — **100%**.

This example is illustrative — each restaurant sets its own minimum order and delivery fees.

## Order smart

<Callout type="default">

- **Check the hours** — the app shows the next opening time if the restaurant is closed, but you save time by checking the slots before building your order.
- **Compare delivery and click & collect** — click & collect removes the delivery fees and lowers the commission (so it supports the restaurant owner more).
- **Use your points** — 1 point = 1 cent of loyalty credit. If you have enough, turn on "Use my points" in the cart to reduce the final amount.
- **Leave clear notes** — specify your exclusions ("no onion") or your preferences in the dish's free-text field — the restaurant will see them.

</Callout>

<Eyebrow icon="error">Mistakes to avoid</Eyebrow>

## Common pitfalls

<Callout type="error">

- **Ordering below the minimum** — if the subtotal doesn't reach the restaurant's threshold, checkout is blocked. Add a dish or choose another restaurant.
- **Changing the address after checkout** — once the order is on its way, you can no longer change the destination. Check **before** paying.
- **Ignoring the statuses** — if an order stays stuck on "Received" for a long time, contact the restaurant (number shown on its page). Tracking is there to alert you.

</Callout>
