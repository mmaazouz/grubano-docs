## The principle (and the why)

Grubano lets restaurant owners duplicate their concept (franchisor) or join a proven network (franchisee), while keeping their customer relationship and loyalty, inside Grubano. You keep your unified dashboard, your sales analytics and your independence — the brand stays with the franchisor, the customer link with the point of sale.

If you run several restaurants, you can [run your network](/en/guides/franchise-operations/) from a single account and track the performance of each brand.

## Submitting an application

<Steps>

### Fill out the application form

Go to the public `/franchise` page and click "Apply". You will need to provide your name, email, motivation and indicate whether you have an equipped kitchen. The SIRET and bank details (RIB) are optional at this stage.

### Select a brand (franchisee) or propose your own (franchisor)

If you apply as a franchisee, choose a brand open to the network from the displayed list (cuisine, average revenue, available cities). If you want to become a franchisor, enter your brand name in the `brandName` field.

### Wait for Grubano's review

Your application moves to `pending` status in the database. The Grubano team reviews your file (presence of a kitchen, project consistency, SIRET if provided) before approving it (`approved`) or rejecting it (`rejected`).

### Access your franchisee area

Once approved, you receive an account with the `franchise` role. You can then log in and access `/franchise/dashboard` to track your points of sale, your orders and your revenue.

</Steps>

## Maximizing your chances of approval

<Callout type="default">
- **Enter your SIRET right from the application** — it speeds up administrative review and proves that you are already registered.
- **Detail your motivation** — explain why this brand (or your own concept) fits your project and your geographic area.
- **Make sure you have an equipped kitchen** — on Grubano, every restaurant cooks in its own premises.
- **Complete your operator profile after approval** — city and phone can be edited later via `PATCH /api/franchise/profile` (these fields do not block approval).
</Callout>

<Eyebrow icon="error">Mistakes to avoid</Eyebrow>

## Common pitfalls

<Callout type="error">
- **Applying without a kitchen** — if you check `hasKitchen: false`, your application may be rejected immediately (Grubano requires every point of sale to cook on site).
- **Sending multiple applications for the same brand** — the `FranchiseeApplication` model enforces a uniqueness constraint on `(restaurantId, brandId)`; a second request for the same pair will fail.
- **Confusing franchising and affiliation** — affiliation (creators / influencers) earns a commission on sales brought in by a referral link; franchising operates a brand and pays a royalty to the franchisor, within the limits set by Grubano.
</Callout>
