# Get Started in 15 Minutes

You are a restaurant owner and want to receive your first orders
today? Expect **~15 minutes** between creating your account and
going live. The rest can be adjusted as you go.

<Callout type="info">
  **You keep your margins.** Grubano commission = 10% per order (vs ~30%
  on traditional platforms). **Your customer data remains yours**:
  Grubano hosts it, does not resell it.
</Callout>

## Before You Begin

Keep on hand:

- A **photo of your menu** (a PDF works too)
- Your **business registration number** and **bank details**
- The **address** and **opening hours**
- (optional) The **logo** + a cover photo

## The 5 Steps

<Steps>

### Create Your Restaurant Account

Go to **[grubano.com](https://www.grubano.com)** → **Pro** tab.
You validate your email, enter your establishment details (business name,
registration number) and access your dashboard. **~2 min.**

### Configure Your Restaurant and Menu

Two things to set up from the dashboard:

- **Restaurant profile** — address, opening hours, average preparation time,
  minimum order amount.
- **Menu** — three options to import your dishes:
  - **AI menu scan** _(recommended for speed)_: photograph
    your menu, the AI recognizes the dishes and suggests name, description, price and
    detected allergens. You correct what’s needed and publish.
    Expect ~5 min for a 30-dish menu.
  - **Manual entry** dish by dish
  - **CSV import** if you already have your catalog in tabular form

### Choose Your Sales Modes

Check the modes you offer:

- **Delivery** — by your delivery staff, by a partner or with delivery
  radius defined on map
- **Click & collect** — online order, pickup on site
- **Table reservation** — floor plan and available time slots

You can enable one, several or all three — adjustable at any time.

### Receive and Manage Your Orders

Switch the restaurant status to **Open** in the dashboard. Orders
arrive in real time on the **Orders** screen: you accept,
you move to preparation, you mark ready → delivered / picked up. A sound
alert notifies you of a new order.

<Callout type="info">
  Pin the tab on a dedicated screen in the kitchen, or use the mobile app:
  the screen stays awake during service.
</Callout>

### (optional) Upgrade to Pro

If you also sell on other platforms (Uber Eats, Deliveroo,
Just Eat…), **Grubano Pro** will **soon** allow you to receive all
these orders directly on your Grubano dashboard, with unified
analytics and reports. €29/month, no commitment. The commission
remains at 10% — Pro is an add-on subscription, it does not affect the
commission.

See [Upgrade to Pro](/en/guides/pro/).

</Steps>
