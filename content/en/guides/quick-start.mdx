# Get started in 15 minutes

You run a restaurant and want to take your first orders today? Count on
**~15 minutes** between creating your account and going live. The rest sorts
itself out as you go.

<Callout type="info">
  **You keep your margins.** Grubano commission of 5% to 12% depending on the
  order mode — delivery 12%, click & collect 8%, dine-in 5% (vs ~30% on the
  legacy platforms). **And you keep your customer relationship and your
  loyalty, in Grubano.**
</Callout>

## Before you start

Keep these handy:

- A **photo of your menu** (a PDF works too)
- Your **SIRET** and your **RIB** (bank details)
- The **address** and **opening hours**
- (optional) The **logo** + a cover photo

## The 5 steps

<Steps>

### Create your restaurant account

Go to **[grubano.com](https://www.grubano.com)** → **Pro** tab. You confirm
your email, enter your restaurant details (legal name, SIRET) and access your
dashboard. **~2 min.**

<Callout type="info">
  **Your restaurant is verified, then published.** When you sign up, Grubano
  automatically verifies your company (SIREN/SIRET) against the official
  registry. Your restaurant is then **pending approval** by the Grubano team
  before it becomes visible to customers. Use this short wait to prepare your
  menu and your order types: everything will be ready the moment you go live.
</Callout>

### Set up your restaurant and menu

Two things to set up from the dashboard:

- **Restaurant profile** — address, opening hours, average preparation time,
  minimum order amount.
- **Menu** — three options to import your dishes:
  - **AI menu scan** _(recommended for speed)_: photograph your menu, the AI
    recognizes the dishes and suggests name, description, price and detected
    allergens. You correct what's needed and publish. Count on ~5 min for a
    30-dish menu.
  - **Manual entry**, dish by dish
  - **CSV import** if you already have your catalog in tabular form

### Choose your order types

Tick the modes you offer:

- **Delivery** — by your own couriers, by a partner or with a delivery radius
  set on the map
- **Click & collect** — order online, pick up at the restaurant
- **Table reservation** — floor plan and available time slots

You can enable one, several or all three — editable at any time.

### Receive and manage your orders

Switch the restaurant status to **Open** in the dashboard. Orders arrive in
real time on the **Orders** screen: you accept, you move to preparation, you
mark ready → delivered / picked up. A sound alert notifies you of a new order.

<Callout type="info">
  Pin the tab to a dedicated screen in the kitchen, or use the mobile app: the
  screen stays awake during service.
</Callout>

### (optional) Go Pro

If you also sell on other platforms (Uber Eats, Deliveroo, Just Eat…),
**Grubano Pro** will **soon** let you receive all these orders directly on your
Grubano dashboard, with unified analytics and reports. 29 €/month, no
commitment. The commission stays the per-mode grid (5% to 12%) — Pro is an
add-on plan, it does not affect the commission.

See [Go Pro](/en/guides/pro/).

</Steps>
