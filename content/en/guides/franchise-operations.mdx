## The principle (and the why)

When you run several restaurants under [franchise](/en/guides/franchise/), Grubano aggregates each location's performance into a single dashboard. There you see the consolidated revenue over the last 30 days, the number of delivered orders, and the amount of royalties owed. Each restaurant appears with its name, its city, its own revenue and a breakdown of its royalties — so you can compare performance and spot the most active locations.

Automated royalty calculation and collection are coming soon: each delivered order will generate a fee based on a rate agreed with you when it opens. The dashboard already tracks three distinct amounts: royalties **accrued** (the total across all periods), **in settlement** (a batch that will be in transfer) and **settled** (the payouts made, once the mechanism is live). This separation will guarantee that no amount is counted twice and that every payout stays traceable.

## View the consolidated reporting

<Steps>

### Open the franchise dashboard

In your workspace, click **Overview** in the sidebar. You immediately see the network's revenue over the last 30 days, compared with the previous period (D-60 → D-30).

### Browse your list of restaurants

Each location appears with its name, its city, its 30-day revenue, the number of delivered orders and the corresponding royalty amount. The list is sorted by opening date.

### Check the financial summary

The top banner shows three totals: **royalties accrued** (the running total since the start), **royalties in settlement** (a batch being prepared for transfer) and **royalties settled** (the completed payouts — the amounts will stay at zero until automated payout is enabled).

### Check the payout history

The **Finances** page (available from the sidebar) will list every payout made, with its date, amount and status, as soon as automated payout is enabled (coming soon). Today you'll already find the breakdown of royalties per restaurant there.

</Steps>

## Common pitfalls

<Callout type="error">
- **Don't confuse the rolling window with the calendar month** — the dashboard shows the last 30 days from today, not the current month. An order from 5 April stays counted until 4 May.
- **Don't add up accrued + in settlement + settled** — these three amounts are stages of a single flow; only royalties accrued represent the all-time total.
- **Don't try to change the rate** — the financial terms are set between the parties involved; contact Grubano for any contractual revision.
</Callout>
